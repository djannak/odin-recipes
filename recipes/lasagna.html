lasagna - here's the recipe.

let's learn how to make the tastiest lasagna you've had in a long time!

We all know that sometimes, making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait - make one of these for your friends and just have everyone asking for the recipe!

A little patience will be needed to make sure everything ends up fine, but don't worry too much about it, just get it started already, everyone's hungry...

necessary ingredients:

alter it as needed to best suit your preferences

  • 1 (one) onion
  • 2 (two) garlic cloves
  • 1 (one) can of crushed tomatos
  • 2 (two) cans tomato sauce
  • 2 (two) cans tomato paste
  • 1 (one) egg
  • meat - any of your preference (a lot of it)
  • sugar
  • spices & seasonings (pick whichever you prefer)
  • lasagna noodles - store bought or homemade
  • cheeses - parmesan, mozarella, ricotta - go wild on these

step by step instructions

here's how to prepare this beautiful dish

  1. make the meat sauce - let all the flavours mix in
  2. let the noodles cook
  3. make the cheese mixes
  4. layer the lasagna - more details on layering down below
  5. cover all with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest a little bit before serving

layering the lasagna

now for the best way to ensure the lasagna ends up perfect!
  1. meat sauce
  2. noodles
  3. ricotta mixture
  4. mozarella slices
  5. meat sauce
  6. parmesan cheese
  7. repeat the layers, top with remaining cheese
