pesto pasta - here's the recipe.

now, let's go ahead and prepare a delicious pesto pasta.

This is the perfect summer salad - use homemade pesto for best results, if you have the patience to do some.

It won't take too much of your time, and you can always replace the vegetables to ones of your preference.

necessary ingredients:

  • pasta (fusilli recommended)
  • cherry tomatoes
  • arugula
  • pesto
  • lemon juice
  • cheese
  • mayonaise
  • seasonings of your preference

step by step instructions

here's how to prepare it - don't worry, it's not that difficult.

  1. cook the pasta - reserve some of the water.
  2. mix all the ingredients in an appropriate sized bowl.
  3. add reserved water to thin out the pesto. Season at gusto.
