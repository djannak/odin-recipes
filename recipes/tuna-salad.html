tuna salad - here's the recipe.

here's the recipe to the perfect tuna salad!

Some tuna salad can never go wrong (unless you're allergic to it) - follow me to see how to prepare this yummy dish!

It won't take too much of your time, I promise.

necessary ingredients:

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1 can baby peas
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 tuna can - drained
  • 1 cup mayonaise

step by step instructions:

here's how to prepare it - real quick.

  1. gather all ingredients.
  2. bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and rinse under cold running water.
  3. mix all the ingredients together.
  4. garnish with egg wedges and a sprinkle of paprika. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
